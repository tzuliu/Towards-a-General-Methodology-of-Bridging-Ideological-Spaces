Data are downloaded from: 

http://www.masaki.j.u-tokyo.ac.jp/utas/utasindex.html

Voters and candidates data in 2009 and 2012 elections are used in the analysis. 
More details are available from the above source (in Japanese).

utas_variable_list_utf-8.csv lists all policy-relevant questions offered in 
2009 and 2012. q_right indicates author-coded ideology of each quesition (1 indicates questions with positive responses implying right-wing ideology; -1 indicates questions with positive responses implying left-wing ideology; NA are ambivalent or non-ideological questions.)
